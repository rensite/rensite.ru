a route through the discography

One voice was born from two.
Then it split apart again.

Linkin Park is convergence and branching. While everyone was busy with the 2000s hits, that part slipped past. Separate roots merged into one band, side projects grew out of it, and after a pause a new voice arrived. Listen in order, and you will hear the whole arc.
